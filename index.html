FAQ

What is included in the $99 visit?

Up to 90 minutes of handyman labor for small repair tasks that can be handled safely with standard tools.

Are materials included?

No. The $99 visit is labor only. Parts, materials, and supplies are paid separately.

What is not included?

Major electrical, plumbing, roofing, structural work, permit work, hazardous repairs, and jobs requiring a licensed specialist are not included.

What if the job takes longer than 90 minutes?

Ken will explain the next step and quote additional time before continuing.

Can I make a list of several small jobs?

Yes. The visit works well for a short punch list. Ken will prioritize what can fit into the visit window.
