Frequently asked questions

Quick answers before you book.

What can I book online?

You can book a $150 Small Repair Visit online for simple small repairs and punch-list items. Larger repairs, installs, painting, and custom work require an in-home estimate.

When are small repair visits available?

Small repair visits are currently available on Sundays.

How do in-home estimates work?

Submit an estimate request with your contact information, address, and a short description of the project. We'll contact you to schedule an in-home visit and provide pricing before work begins.

Do I need to upload photos?

No. Photos are optional. They may help us understand the repair, but larger jobs are priced after an in-home estimate.

Do you handle painting?

Yes. Painting requires an in-home estimate and is quoted separately based on the size, prep work, materials, and condition of the area.

What areas do you serve?

Serving New Orleans, Harvey, Marrero, Gretna, Westwego, Metairie, Kenner, Belle Chasse, and nearby West Bank neighborhoods.

Do you handle licensed trade work?

Fix-It Home Services focuses on small handyman repairs, adjustments, minor installs, and punch-list jobs. If a job requires a licensed trade, permit, or larger project quote, we'll tell you before booking.

Is the work guaranteed?

We stand behind our workmanship. If something we repaired or installed is not right, contact us and we'll make it right. The guarantee applies to our labor, not customer-provided materials, product defects, or new damage.
